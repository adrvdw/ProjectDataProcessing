Project

Problem


Before 2017, cryptocurrencies were relatively unknown among normal people. Almost no one had ever heard of cryptocurrecies. In 2017, Bitcoin showed a strong growth and set a record above $20.000. All of a sudden, cryptocurrencies were all over the news, and all coins increased in value. This apparant eternal growth suddenly stopped, and all currencies began to depreciate, until this day. The problem is therefore that cryptocurrencies lack the usual fundamental metrics investors would look for when attempting to assign an appropriate value to an asset.

Solution

Never has the past learned so much about trends as with cryptocurrencies. Looking for trends in the past can help to decide invest in cryptocurrencies and predict future expectations. This site shows therefore trends with time of all large cryptocurrencies.
